Legends of Chaos

	

What is LoC?

	
LoC is a text based role playing game, where 
any character or style of story you'd 
like can come to life. With simple rules of battle 
and stats, with as little math as possible, it aims 
to be a game that can be easily enjoyed by 
anyone who'd like to play a casual role playing game.
